# Renations – Factions

The world ended in **2052**.

What began as humanity’s greatest scientific achievement triggered a chain of events that led to global war, nuclear winter, and the collapse of civilization. Generations later, the survivors of that catastrophe have evolved into very different societies.

These factions now struggle for survival, power, and control over the reborn world.

---

# Time Travelers

**Motto:** *“From time’s edge, we rebuild.”*

**Short Description**  
Scientists from the past who accidentally triggered the apocalypse while attempting humanity’s first time jump. Stranded in a ruined future, they now use their advanced knowledge to survive and reshape the world.

## Lore

For centuries humanity dreamed of conquering time.

Physicists declared it impossible. Philosophers called it madness. But human history had always followed the same pattern: the impossible simply waited long enough to become inevitable.

In **2011**, deep beneath the mountains of Europe, scientists at **CERN** recreated a microscopic echo of the Big Bang. In the aftermath of the experiment they discovered something extraordinary—**antimatter** that could bend gravity and distort spacetime.

The discovery led to the creation of the **Secret Institute of Time and Space (SITP)**. The greatest scientific minds were secretly gathered to build the first time machine.

After decades of work and the invention of powerful antimatter–fusion reactors, the impossible became reality.

In **2052**, the machine—named **Aion**—was activated. Hundreds of experts were sent forward in time to witness humanity’s predicted Golden Age.

Moments after launch the energy containment pillars failed.

The resulting explosion destroyed nearby cities and triggered global panic. Nations blamed each other. War erupted, nuclear weapons were launched, and within months the world collapsed into nuclear winter.

When the time travelers finally arrived in the future, they found only ruins.

With no way to return home, survival became their only goal. Over time, disagreements split the travelers into rival groups, each determined to rebuild the world in their own vision.

# Myrmidons

**Motto:** *“From the deep, we rise.”*

**Short Description**  
Genetically altered humans abandoned beneath the oceans during the apocalypse. Over generations they evolved into a powerful aquatic civilization inspired by ancient warrior myths.

## Lore

Before the fall of civilization, humanity looked to the oceans as the next frontier.

Scientists began experimenting with genetic mutation technology to create humans capable of surviving deep underwater. In **2050**, the first successful ocean colonies were established and thousands volunteered to become part of this new underwater civilization.

Then the apocalypse came.

In **2052**, communication with the surface world suddenly ended. Soon after, the oceans began freezing as nuclear winter spread across the planet. Within months a massive layer of ice sealed the seas.

The underwater colonies were trapped.

Abandoned by the surface world, the ocean dwellers adapted and evolved. Generations later they no longer saw themselves as human.

They became the **Myrmidons**.

Their culture grew around ancient myths of Greece and Rome—stories of warriors, gods, and empires beneath the sea. But as their civilization expanded, rival beliefs divided them.

Now the children of the deep prepare to rise from the frozen oceans and claim their place in the new world.

# The Order

**Motto:** *“Order through power.”*

**Short Description**  
Descendants of an ancient secret society that survived the apocalypse in hidden Antarctic bunkers. Now their noble houses return to the surface to conquer and rebuild civilization.

## Lore

When the world fell in **2052**, it was not entirely by accident.

A secret society known as **The Order of the Knights Templar** had spent centuries influencing world events from the shadows. Believing humanity required strong guidance, they manipulated global tensions until nuclear war became inevitable.

While the world burned, the Order had already prepared their sanctuary.

Deep beneath the Antarctic ice they built massive underground cities—human arks designed to preserve those they considered worthy.

The survivors lived under strict hierarchy. An **Emperor** ruled with the support of powerful noble houses while generations passed beneath the frozen continent.

Eventually the nuclear winter ended.

As the surface became habitable again, rebellion erupted within the underground empire. The Emperor was assassinated and the noble houses turned against each other.

Now the descendants of the Order march back into the world above.

Each house seeks land, power, and dominion over the reborn Earth.

# Children of the Prophecy

**Motto:** *“We are the guardians of Aevum.”*

**Short Description**  
A secretive spiritual community that survived the apocalypse in isolation. After generations of meditation and evolution, they return to the surface with powerful psychic abilities.

## Lore

Just before the apocalypse of **2052**, a wealthy visionary named **William Grates** claimed to have seen the future.

In his vision, a voice warned him of a coming catastrophe and declared that humanity must evolve beyond greed and destruction.

Grates gathered followers who believed in the prophecy and built a hidden underground sanctuary.

Weeks later the world ended.

For generations the isolated community studied philosophy, spirituality, and discipline. They believed their purpose was to prepare for the rebirth of the world.

Over time something extraordinary happened.

Their descendants developed unusual mental abilities—telepathy, illusions, and heightened awareness.

They believed this transformation marked the next step in human evolution.

When they finally returned to the surface, they discovered that humanity had begun rebuilding once again.

Now the **Children of the Prophecy** walk the wastelands, determined to guide the new world and prevent the mistakes of the past from repeating.
